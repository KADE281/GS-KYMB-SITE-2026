Important Information for Applicants

New Applicants

If you're applying for the first time, click "Create Account" above to register. You'll need a valid email address and phone number. After creating your account, you can access the enrollment application form from your dashboard.

What You'll Need

Before starting your application, prepare: Birth certificate, academic certificates/result slips, passport photos (2), parent/guardian ID, and transfer letter (if applicable). Have digital copies ready for upload.

Your Account Security

Keep your login credentials safe. Your account allows you to track your application status, view admission decisions, download receipts, and access important documents. Never share your password with anyone.

Need Help?

Having trouble creating an account or applying? Contact our admissions office at admissions@gskayumbu.edu or call +250 788 000 000. We're here Monday-Friday, 8 AM - 5 PM.
