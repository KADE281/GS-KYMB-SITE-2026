About Us

Discover our story, values, and commitment to excellence in education

Our Vision

To be a leading international institution of academic excellence, dedicated to the generation and dissemination of knowledge that transforms lives and drives national development. We envision a future where every student who walks through our doors becomes a confident, skilled, and ethical leader capable of making meaningful contributions to society and the global community.

Our vision extends beyond academic achievement to encompass the holistic development of each student. We aspire to create an educational environment where innovation thrives, critical thinking is nurtured, and students are empowered to become agents of positive change in their communities and beyond. Through our commitment to excellence, we aim to be recognized not only for our academic standards but also for our contribution to building a knowledgeable, skilled, and morally upright generation that will shape Rwanda's future and contribute to global progress.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality education, research, and community service while fostering a culture of innovation, integrity, and lifelong learning for our students. We are committed to creating an inclusive learning environment that nurtures critical thinking, creativity, and character development. Through dedicated teaching, modern facilities, and comprehensive support systems, we prepare our students not just for examinations, but for life's challenges and opportunities.

Our mission is realized through a multi-faceted approach that combines rigorous academic instruction with practical skills development, particularly through our Technical and Vocational (TSS) programs. We believe in providing personalized attention to each student, recognizing their unique talents and learning styles. Our dedicated faculty members employ innovative teaching methodologies, integrate technology into learning, and create engaging classroom experiences that inspire curiosity and foster a love for learning. We are committed to maintaining high standards of academic excellence while ensuring that our students develop strong moral values, social responsibility, and the confidence to pursue their dreams and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Story

Founded to serve the nation's intellectual needs, GS KAYUMBU/TSS has grown from a small institution into one of Rwanda's premier centers for secondary education. Our journey began with a vision to provide quality education that combines academic excellence with technical and vocational skills, preparing students not only for higher education but also for immediate entry into the workforce with practical, marketable skills.

Over the years, we have remained steadfast in our commitment to educational excellence, continuously adapting our programs to meet the evolving needs of our students and society. What started as a modest educational facility has blossomed into a comprehensive institution serving over 800 students, offering diverse programs from O-Level through A-Level, complemented by our specialized Technical and Vocational (TSS) programs in Fashion and Design (FAD) and Building and Construction (BDC).

Establishment

Our Beginning

Started with a small group of dedicated educators and students, committed to building a better future through education. Our founders envisioned a school that would not only impart knowledge but also build character, instill values, and prepare students for the challenges of the modern world.

Growth

Expansion & Development

Expanded our facilities, introduced TSS programs, and grew to serve over 800 students with diverse academic and technical offerings. We invested in modern laboratories, computer facilities, and specialized workshops for our technical programs. Our teaching staff grew to include over 50 qualified educators, many with advanced degrees and specialized training in their fields.

Today

Excellence in Education

Now recognized as a leading institution offering O-Level, A-Level, and Technical programs with modern facilities and qualified staff. We maintain a strong track record of academic success, with our students consistently performing well in national examinations and gaining admission to prestigious universities and technical colleges. Our graduates are making their mark in various fields, from engineering and medicine to fashion design and construction management.
