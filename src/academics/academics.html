Academics

Welcome to GS KAYUMBU/TSS - Where academic excellence meets innovation. Since our establishment, we've been dedicated to transforming lives through quality education, empowering students with knowledge, skills, and values that transcend the classroom. Our comprehensive curriculum blends traditional learning with modern teaching methodologies, ensuring every student receives personalized attention and guidance. Join us in and embark on an educational journey that prepares you not just for exams, but for life's greatest challenges and opportunities ahead.

Academic Programs

Ordinary Level (O-Level)

Forms 1-3: Foundation education covering core subjects preparing students for national exams.

  • Mathematics
  • Sciences
  • Languages
  • Social Studies

Advanced Level (A-Level)

Forms 4-6: Specialized education in chosen combinations preparing for university.

  • MCE - Math, Computer, Economics

Technical & Vocational (TSS)

Practical skills training in technical fields for career readiness.

  • FAD - Fashion and Design
  • BDC - Building and Construction
