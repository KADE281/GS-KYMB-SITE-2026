Important Announcements

Leadership & Professional Conduct

As educators and leaders, you are the backbone of our institution. Your dedication shapes the future of our students. Remember that excellence in education begins with your commitment to discipline, respect, and professionalism. Uphold the values of integrity, punctuality, and accountability in all your duties.

Responsibilities & Expectations

Every role in our institution is vital. Teachers: Prepare engaging lessons and assess students fairly. DOS: Coordinate academic programs effectively. Accountants: Maintain accurate financial records. All staff: Attend meetings punctually, submit reports on time, and collaborate with colleagues to create a positive learning environment.

Respect & Collaboration

Treat students, parents, and colleagues with respect and dignity. Foster a culture of teamwork and open communication. Address conflicts professionally and support one another in achieving our shared mission of educational excellence. Your positive attitude influences the entire school community.

Staff Portal Guide

New Staff Members

Create your account using your official school email. Select your role (Teacher, DOS, Accountant, etc.) and complete your profile. Contact HR if you need assistance with registration.

Dashboard Access

Once logged in, access your personalized dashboard to manage classes, view schedules, submit reports, track student progress, and communicate with administration.

Account Security

Keep your login credentials confidential. Use a strong password and never share your account. Log out after each session, especially on shared computers.
