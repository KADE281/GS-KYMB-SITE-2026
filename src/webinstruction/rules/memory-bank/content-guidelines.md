# Content Guidelines & Standards

## Brand Voice & Tone
- **Professional yet approachable**: Maintains academic credibility while being welcoming
- **Inspirational**: Focuses on student potential and future success
- **Inclusive**: Welcomes students from all backgrounds
- **Achievement-oriented**: Highlights success stories and accomplishments

## Key Messaging
- "Igniting Knowledge, Inspiring Innovation" (Main tagline)
- Focus on academic excellence and practical skills
- Emphasis on holistic development
- Community and national development themes

## Content Sections

### Homepage
- Hero section with compelling call-to-action
- Statistics: 800+ students, 6 partners, 15+ years
- Vision, Mission, Core Values preview
- Quick access to all major sections

### About Page
- Comprehensive school history and background
- Detailed vision and mission statements
- Core values with explanations
- Leadership team information
- Timeline of school development
- Achievements and recognition

### Academics
- Program descriptions (O-Level, A-Level, TSS)
- Curriculum information
- Teaching methodology
- Academic facilities
- Resources and downloads section

### Admissions
- Program requirements
- Application process (6-step timeline)
- Required documents checklist
- Important dates (annually updated)
- Fee structure
- FAQ section

## Administrative Notes
- Dates in admissions section require annual updates
- Fee information needs regular review
- Leadership team photos and names to be updated
- Contact information should be verified regularly
