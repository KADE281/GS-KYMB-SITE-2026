Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to join our school

1

Create Student Account

Sign up on the Student Portal to create your account. This gives you access to apply and track your application.

2

Login to Portal

Login to your Student Portal account to access the enrollment application form.

3

Submit Application

Fill out the enrollment form with accurate information and upload required documents.

4

Application Review

Our admissions team reviews your application (2-5 business days).

5

Admission Decision

Check your portal for admission decision. You'll also receive notification via email or phone.

6

Complete Enrollment

Pay enrollment fees through your portal and download your registration receipt and documents.

Frequently Asked Questions

When does the application period open?

Applications open on January 15, 2025 and close on August 30, 2025. We encourage early application as spaces are limited.

Can I apply if I'm transferring from another school?

Yes! Transfer students are welcome. You'll need a transfer letter from your previous school along with your academic records.

How long does the admission process take?

Applications are typically reviewed within 2-5 business days. You'll receive notification via email or phone.

Are scholarships available?

We offer limited scholarships based on academic merit and financial need. Contact our admissions office for more information.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Mobile Money (MTN/Airtel), bank transfers, and cash payments at the school office.

Can I visit the school before applying?

Absolutely! School tours are available Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 4 PM. Contact us to schedule a visit.
